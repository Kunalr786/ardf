About Our Foundation

Our Mission

Founded in 1997 and headquartered in Nandurbar, Maharashtra, the Adivasi Research and Development Foundation has been at the forefront of community development for over two decades. Our mission is to empower marginalized communities, particularly the Adivasi population, through comprehensive development programs.

Our Approach

We believe in sustainable development that respects traditional knowledge while embracing modern solutions. Our approach combines research, community participation, and innovative implementation to create lasting change.

Recognition

Recognized by both Central and State governments of India, we have received numerous accolades for our dedication to social transformation and community empowerment.

Our Programs

Healthcare

Providing accessible healthcare services, medical camps, and health awareness programs to remote communities. Our mobile health units reach the most underserved areas with qualified medical professionals.

Learn More

Women Empowerment

Empowering women through skill development, microfinance, leadership training, and advocacy programs. Building stronger communities through women's participation and economic independence.

Learn More

Sports & Culture

Promoting traditional sports, cultural preservation, and youth development through recreational activities, cultural festivals, and talent nurturing programs.

Learn More

Water & Sanitation

Implementing water conservation projects, building sanitation facilities, and promoting hygiene awareness in rural communities for better health outcomes.
